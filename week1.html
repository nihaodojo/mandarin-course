Week 1: Introduction to Mandarin and Pinyin

🎯 Objectives: Learn the basics of Pinyin and tones.

🎓 What is Pinyin?

Pinyin is the phonetic system used to pronounce Chinese characters using the Latin alphabet.

🎵 The Four Tones

  • 1st Tone (High Level): mā (妈 – mother)
  • 2nd Tone (Rising): má (麻 – hemp)
  • 3rd Tone (Falling-Rising): mǎ (马 – horse)
  • 4th Tone (Falling): mà (骂 – scold)

📝 Practice

Repeat these words aloud:

  • 你好 (nǐ hǎo) – Hello
  • 再见 (zài jiàn) – Goodbye
  • 我叫 (wǒ jiào) – I am called...
